Oterre

Oterre is a design duo comprising of Charis Alexander and Jennifer Choi, Parsons The New School for Design alumni based in NYC. Created from a shared love for design, fashion and technology, the first collection centers around a concept of play and introducing it into the mundane. 

Accessories are a daily part of modern human life – the simple habit of subtly changing one's appearance influences both personal and external impressions. This act of customization is celebrated through the use of 3D printing as a method for rapidly creating lightweight, wearable designs inspired by elemental shapes and crisp outlines. When juxtaposed with rich materials, delicate pendants lend an air of sophistication.
